Located at two campuses in France (Paris and Lille) and with English as a teaching language, IESEG School of Management is an internationally oriented and highly rigorous school with a strong commitment to social responsibility. Since its launch in 1964, step by step IESEG has emerged as one of the leading business and management education providers in France. Top school status is assured by “Triple Crown Accreditation” (EQUIS, AACSB, and AMBA along with French Accreditation of the Conférence des Grandes Ecoles) and solid positions in international rankings (Master in Management ranked the 32nd globally by the Financial Time, ranked among Top 10 business schools in France by L’Etudiant, Le Figaro, and QS World University Rankings). IESEG has more than 5 500 students of 100+ nationalities. It’s modern campus in Paris is located in the iconic Grande Arche building, at the heart of the business district of La Défense.
